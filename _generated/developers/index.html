Danny Del Grosso

Who is he?

Danny Del Grosso is a Full Stack Website Developer based out of Philadelphia, PA. He attended Indiana University of Pennsylvania to get his Bachelors of Science in Management Information Systems. He now works for a small web dev firm in Lansdale, PA called eNet Partners. He mainly codes in C# and VB.Net but he really enjoys coding in javascript.

Him and Andrew decided to make a CMS website for the Ultimate Frisbee team since they were both proud members. This CMS is built from a CMS called Enduro.js. It has a full Admin backend for any content management needs! And it does it all with static webpages built from Handlebars templating. Pretty neat stuff!
